Test result:

HPV not found

A negative test result means that HPV has not been found in your cervix.

What this means

This means your risk of getting cervical cancer is very low. You do not need a smear test for now to check for abnormal cervical cells, even if you have had these in the past.

No test is 100% effective. In extremely rare cases, tests may fail to pick up an HPV infection that needs further investigation. This is called a ‘false negative’ result. If you have any worry symptoms please visit your healthcare provider.

Next Steps

Tests are due every 3 years. We will contact you in 3 years to remind you.
